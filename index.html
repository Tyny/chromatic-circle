Chromatic Circle

Twelve semitones. Tap a note to hear it above the root; double-tap to make it the new root. Press m for minor, M for major.

Root C major
Shaded bands group the interval families — 2nds, 3rds, 6ths, 7ths (each a minor/major pair). The root, perfect 4th & 5th and tritone are left unshaded.
